World's Best Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish known for its rich layers of pasta, savory meat or vegetable fillings, creamy bechamel sauce, and melted cheese, often mozzarella and Parmesan. Each layer is carefully stacked, creating a hearty and satisfying meal that is baked to perfection, resulting in a golden, bubbly top. This versatile dish can be customized with a variety of ingredients, such as ricotta cheese, spinach, mushrooms, or a robust meat sauce, making it a beloved favorite at family gatherings and celebrations. Lasagna's comforting flavors and textures have made it a staple in Italian cuisine and popular around the world.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
